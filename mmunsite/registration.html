Main Conference

The main conference of Manila International Model United Nations is a four day hybrid event in November 11-14 that will host 5 councils overall. Two councils are offline while three councils are online. The offline events will have free food, and other amenities and accessories provided by the organizers. Both councils will host a socials night and an opening and closing ceremony, with guests from the Philippines and around the world. We hope to officially mark the start and tenure of Manila International Model United Nations as a premier Model UN conference from the Philippines, and wish to bring more attention towards this Model UN by establishing our reputation of being a host for high quality conferences.
